My Favorite Villagers

Hello everyone, its dani back at it again with another opinion section. Today I will be discussing my opinions on the animal crossing villagers that I have had on my acnh island. I will be ranking them from worst to best and giving them a tier

Leopold

God where do I even start with this awful creation. Leopold is literally the worst thing I have laid my eyes upon. Everyday I would go water my flowers, talk to my other villagers, and then come across Leopold. I was faced with 2 options: Ignore him, or hit him with my net (or option 3: push him into a pitfall seed). You can guess which option I usually chose. Then, I would go to Isabelle and complain about how much I wanted Leopold to leave my island. Every day spent with Leopold on my island was comparable to a day spent in hell. Every day since Leopold left has been a blessing. I don't dislike Leopold for no good reason. No, look at that face, the disdain he casts upon everyone he sees. Leopold is not just a smug type villager, nintendo just didn't know what type to give him and gave him the closest thing.Instead, Leopold is a demon type villager, a villager who emerged from the deepest layer of hell to taunt everyone who was cursed with him coming to their island.

Rating: -100/ 10

tier: Z
